Pizza Hawaii

Description

The Hawaiian Pizza is a controversial yet popular choice that combines sweet and savory flavors. It is topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, ham, and pineapple.

The sweetness of the pineapple complements the saltiness of the ham, creating a unique flavor profile.

Special note: I'm Italian so this pizza is an insult to me, sadly this is my wife's favorite pizza..

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Pineapple

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough.
  2. Spread the tomate sauce.
  3. Add the mozzarella.
  4. Add the Pineapple.
