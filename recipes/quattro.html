Pizza Quattro Formaggi

Description

The Quattro Formaggi (Four Cheese) Pizza is a cheese lover's delight. It features a blend of four different cheeses, usually including mozzarella, gorgonzola, ricotta, and parmesan.

The combination of these cheeses creates a rich, creamy, and flavorful pizza. It can be made with either a tomato sauce base or a white (no tomato) base.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Add three other cheeses (gorgonzola, ricotta, parmesan)

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough.
  2. Spread the tomate sauce.
  3. Add the mozzarella.
  4. Add three other cheeses (gorgonzola, ricotta, parmesan).
